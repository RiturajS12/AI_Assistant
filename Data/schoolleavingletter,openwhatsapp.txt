**School Leaving Letter**

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, State, Pincode]
[Date]

[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Designation]
[School Name]
[School Address]
[City, State, Pincode]

Respected [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to inform you that I, [Your Name], a student of [Class/Grade] at [School Name], wish to leave the school with immediate effect. My last day of attendance will be [Date].

I appreciate the opportunities that I have received during my time at [School Name], and I am grateful for the support and guidance provided by the teachers and staff. However, due to [briefly mention the reason for leaving, e.g. family relocation, health reasons, etc.], I am forced to take this decision.

I will make sure to complete all my pending assignments and projects before my departure. I will also ensure that all my dues are cleared, including any outstanding fees or library books.

Please let me know if there are any formalities that need to be completed before I can officially leave the school. I would appreciate your cooperation in this matter.

Thank you for your understanding.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]
