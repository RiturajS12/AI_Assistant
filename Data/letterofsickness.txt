Here is a sample letter of sickness:

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, State, Pincode]
[Date]

[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Title]
[Company/Organization Name]
[Company Address]
[City, State, Pincode]

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to inform you that I will be unable to attend work/school for the next [number] days due to health reasons. Unfortunately, I am currently suffering from [briefly mention the illness or medical condition, e.g. fever, flu, etc.].

My doctor has advised me to take complete bed rest and undergo treatment for the next [number] days to ensure a speedy recovery. I am not feeling well enough to perform my duties and do not want to risk infecting my colleagues.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will do my best to catch up on any missed work as soon as I am feeling better. I will keep you updated on my progress and provide a medical certificate if required.

Please let me know if there are any pressing tasks that need my attention in the meantime, and I will do my best to complete them remotely.

Thank you for your understanding and support during this time.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

Please note that this is a sample letter and you should customize it as per your needs and circumstances. Also, be sure to keep a polite and professional tone throughout the letter.
